THE TIME I DO MY DAILY ACTIVITIES

Let me tell you the time I do my daily activities.
First, I wake-up at five o’clock in the morning.
Then, I take a bath and brush my teeth at a quarter past five.
After that, I have breakfast at a half past five.
I go to school at six o’clock.
I study in class at a half past six in the morning.
I have lunch at a quarter past twelve in the afternoon.
I go home from school at two o’clock in the afternoon.
Then, I play with my friends at four o’clock in the afternoon.
I have dinner with my family at a half past six in the evening.
I do my homework at eight o’clock in the evening.
Finally, I go to bed at nine o’clock in the evening.
I’m always on time.
