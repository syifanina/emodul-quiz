"Guess the Activity" Game

Play the game with your friend! Pick one of the card! Pretend to do the hobby in the card. Ask your friend “What is the hobby?” Say if your friend’s guessing is right or wrong. Look at the example!

Mainkan bersama teman! Pilihlah salah satu kartu! Peragakan hoby yang digambarkan dalam kartu tadi. Tanyakan kepada temanmu “Tebak apa hobinya?” Katakan jika tebakan temanmu benar atau salah! Perhatikan contoh!
