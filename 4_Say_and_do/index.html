WHAT TIME IS IT?

Say the time and move your hands like a watch!

  • Right hand = minute watch’s hand
  • Left hand = hour watch’s hand
  • Look at the example!

Ucapkan waktu sesuai gambar dan gerakkan tanganmu seolah jadi jarum.

Tangan kanan = jarum menit (jarum panjang)
Tangan kiri = jarum jam (jarum pendek). Perhatikan contoh!
