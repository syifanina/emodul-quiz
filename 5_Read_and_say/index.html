SHOW ME PARTS OF YOUR HOUSE

It is Sunday.
Made is at Firman's house.
"Firman, can you show me parts of your house?" says Made.
"Ok, this is our living room," says Firman.
"Sit down on the sofa in the living room," says Firman.
"Then, this is my bedroom," says Firman.
"I sleep in my bedroom," says Firman.
"Show me your kitchen, Firman," says Made.
"Ok, my mother is in the kitchen," says Firman.
"Good afternoon, Mrs. Rahmah," says Made.
"Good afternoon, Made," says Mrs. Rahmah.
"I baked a cake for you," says Mrs. Rahmah.
"Eat the cake in the dining room with Firman," says Mrs. Rahmah.
"Thank you," says Made.
"Sorry, I have to go to the laundry room," says Mrs. Rahmah.
"I iron the clothes in the laundry room," says Mrs. Rahmah.
"Now, let's go to the study room," says Firman.
"Read the books in the study room," says Firman.
After that, they move to the terrace.
"Play with the cats on the terrace," says Firman.
"Okay, Firman," says Made.
"Before you go, get your bike in the garage," says Firman.
"I put it there," says Firman.
"Thanks, Firman," says Made.
"You have a very nice house," says Made.
